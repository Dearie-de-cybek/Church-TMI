Prophetic Affirmation for Kingdom Heralds

"I am a voice ordained in eternity, awakened for such a time as this and generations to come. The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, for He has anointed me to herald the message of the King. I do not go in my own strength, but in the fire of His presence.

My tongue is like a ready pen, inscribing truth upon hearts. I decree what heaven declares. I proclaim liberty to captives, light to those in darkness, and the nearness of the Kingdom. I am sent—not by man, but by the Lord of Hosts. I walk in divine alignment, clothed in righteousness, armed with truth, and crowned with purpose.

The sound of awakening is in my mouth. I will not be silent. I am a herald of the King."

I AM A DISCIPLE OF CHRIST

— Unknown Missionary (The Fellowship of the Unashamed)

I am part of the fellowship of the unashamed. I have Holy Spirit power. The die has been cast. I have stepped over the line. The decision has been made—I'm a disciple of His.

I won't look back, let up, slow down, back away, or be still. My past is redeemed, my present makes sense, my future is secure. I'm finished and done with low living, sight walking, small planning, smooth knees, colorless dreams, tamed visions, worldly talking, cheap giving, and dwarfed goals.

I no longer need preeminence, prosperity, position, promotions, plaudits, or popularity. I don't have to be right, first, tops, recognized, praised, regarded, or rewarded. I now live by faith, lean on His presence, walk by patience, am uplifted by prayer, and labor with power.

My face is set, my gait is fast, my goal is heaven, my road is narrow, my way rough, my companions few, my Guide reliable, my mission clear.

I cannot be bought, compromised, detoured, lured away, turned back, deluded, or delayed. I will not flinch in the face of sacrifice, hesitate in the presence of adversity, negotiate at the table of the enemy, ponder at the pool of popularity, or meander in the maze of mediocrity.

I won't give up, shut up, let up, until I've stayed up, stored up, prayed up, paid up, preached up for the cause of Christ.

I am a disciple of Jesus. I must go till He comes, give till I drop, preach till all know, and work till He stops me. And when He comes for His own, He will have no problem recognizing me. My banner will be clear!

Our Vision & Mission

To raise a generation of Spirit-filled believers who are deeply rooted in the Word, actively engaged in ministry, and bold in proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.

01

Biblical Foundation

Build a strong theological and scriptural foundation for effective ministry

02

Character Development

Cultivate Christlike character and spiritual maturity in every participant

03

Ministry Skills

Equip with practical ministry skills including evangelism, preaching, and discipleship

04

Kingdom Impact

Deploy trained heralds to impact communities and advance God's Kingdom

12-Week Curriculum

A comprehensive journey through three transformative months of intensive discipleship training

MONTH 1

FOUNDATION

Laying the Groundwork for Ministry

Week 1

The Call to Herald

  • Identity and purpose as Kingdom Heralds
  • Biblical examples of God's messengers
  • Understanding your divine mandate
Week 2

The Message of the Kingdom

  • What is the Kingdom of God?
  • Gospel presentation and articulation
  • Core doctrines of Christianity
Week 3

The Power of the Word

  • Scripture memorization techniques
  • Inductive Bible study methods
  • Rightly dividing the Word of truth
Week 4

The Holy Spirit & Prayer

  • The Person and work of the Holy Spirit
  • Developing a powerful prayer life
  • Hearing God's voice clearly
MONTH 2

FORMATION

Character & Practical Ministry Skills

Week 5

Character of a Herald

  • Integrity, humility, and faithfulness
  • Overcoming personal obstacles
  • Walking in holiness
Week 6

Evangelism & Soul Winning

  • Sharing your testimony effectively
  • Street evangelism practicals
  • Answering tough questions about faith
Week 7

Preaching & Teaching

  • Sermon preparation and delivery
  • Communicating with clarity and power
  • Using illustrations and storytelling
Week 8

Worship & Intercession

  • Leading others into God's presence
  • Prophetic worship and prayer
  • Spiritual warfare basics
MONTH 3

DEPLOYMENT

Activation & Commission

Week 9

Discipleship & Mentorship

  • Making disciples who make disciples
  • Shepherding and accountability
  • Building spiritual families
Week 10

Ministry in the Marketplace

  • Workplace ministry strategies
  • Balancing secular work and ministry

Field Ministry Practicals

Commissioning & Graduation

  • Final presentations and testimonies
