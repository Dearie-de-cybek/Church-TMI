Prophetic Affirmation for Kingdom Heralds

"I am a voice ordained in eternity, awakened for such a time as this and generations to come. The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, for He has anointed me to herald the message of the King. I do not go in my own strength, but in the fire of His presence.

My tongue is like a ready pen, inscribing truth upon hearts. I decree what heaven declares. I proclaim liberty to captives, light to those in darkness, and the nearness of the Kingdom. I am sent—not by man, but by the Lord of Hosts. I walk in divine alignment, clothed in righteousness, armed with truth, and crowned with purpose.

The sound of awakening is in my mouth. I will not be silent. I am a herald of the King."

I AM A DISCIPLE OF CHRIST

— Unknown Missionary (The Fellowship of the Unashamed)

I am part of the fellowship of the unashamed. I have Holy Spirit power. The die has been cast. I have stepped over the line. The decision has been made—I'm a disciple of His.

I won't look back, let up, slow down, back away, or be still. My past is redeemed, my present makes sense, my future is secure. I'm finished and done with low living, sight walking, small planning, smooth knees, colorless dreams, tamed visions, worldly talking, cheap giving, and dwarfed goals.

I no longer need preeminence, prosperity, position, promotions, plaudits, or popularity. I don't have to be right, first, tops, recognized, praised, regarded, or rewarded. I now live by faith, lean on His presence, walk by patience, am uplifted by prayer, and labor with power.

My face is set, my gait is fast, my goal is heaven, my road is narrow, my way rough, my companions few, my Guide reliable, my mission clear.

I cannot be bought, compromised, detoured, lured away, turned back, deluded, or delayed. I will not flinch in the face of sacrifice, hesitate in the presence of adversity, negotiate at the table of the enemy, ponder at the pool of popularity, or meander in the maze of mediocrity.

I won't give up, shut up, let up, until I've stayed up, stored up, prayed up, paid up, preached up for the cause of Christ.

I am a disciple of Jesus. I must go until He comes, give until I drop, preach until all know, and work until He stops me.

And when He comes for His own, He'll have no problem recognizing me—My banner will be clear!

Program Vision

To raise heralds who understand, embody, and proclaim the message of God's Kingdom with boldness, purity, and authority in every sphere of engagement.

Theoretical

Deep theological foundations and Kingdom principles

Practical

Hands-on ministry experience and activation

Spiritual Formation

Character development and Christ-likeness

Session Objectives

Each weekly session is designed to help participants:

1

Gain foundational understanding of the Kingdom of God

2

Develop strong spiritual disciplines and Christ-like character

3

Cultivate sensitivity to the Holy Spirit and prophetic insight

4

Learn practical ministry and leadership skills

5

Experience activation through hands-on ministry

6

Discover and articulate their personal Kingdom mandate

12-Week Training Curriculum

MONTH 1

Foundation & Identity

Week 1

Kingdom Perspectives

  • Overview of the Kingdom of God
  • The King, His Kingdom & Covenant
  • Biblical Authority and Sound Doctrine
Week 2

Spiritual Formation & Discipleship

  • Identity in Christ & Sonship
  • Intimacy with God
  • Spiritual Disciplines & Devotion
Week 3

Prayer & Intercession

  • Foundations of Effective Prayer
  • Intercession & Watchman Ministry
  • Fasting & Spiritual Sensitivity
Week 4

Worship & Creative Expression

  • Redefining Worship
  • Prophetic Worship & Atmosphere Shifting
  • Using Music, Drama & Arts in Heralding

MONTH 2

Equipping & Empowering

Week 5

Prophetic Ministry

  • The Prophetic Call of a Herald
  • Hearing and Interpreting God's Voice
  • Operating in the Gifts of the Spirit
Week 6

Evangelism & Missions

  • Kingdom Evangelism Models
  • Power Evangelism & Testimony
  • Cross-cultural Witnessing & Global Perspective
Week 7

Apostolic Leadership & Governance

  • Fivefold Ministry and the Role of Heralds
  • Leadership Ethics & Kingdom Authority
  • Building Apostolic Teams & Structures
Week 8

Culture & Reformation

  • Influencing the 7 Mountains of Culture
  • Marketplace Ministry
  • Media & Kingdom Messaging

MONTH 3

Activation & Commissioning

Week 9

Practicum – Ministry Simulation

  • Street Evangelism
  • Prophetic & Intercessory Sessions
  • Worship Nights & Preaching Labs
Week 10

Mentoring & Accountability

  • Small Groups & Discipleship
  • Conflict Resolution & Humility in Ministry
  • Building Healthy Relationships in Ministry
Week 11

Strategy & Vision Casting

  • Crafting Personal Mandate as a Herald
  • Building Ministry Vision Boards
  • Navigating Opposition & Resistance
Week 12

Commissioning & Graduation

  • Final Assessment & Presentations
  • Prophetic Impartation & Anointing Service
  • Commissioning as Kingdom Heralds

Assessment Methods

Participants will be assessed through:

Weekly Reflection Journals

Personal takeaways and insights

Group Projects

Evangelism, worship, and prophetic activation

Ministry Simulations

Role-playing and real-time ministry tasks

Peer Reviews

Constructive feedback within small accountability groups

Final Presentation

Articulation of personal mandate and vision

Faculty Evaluation

Based on participation, growth, and engagement
