The values we live by .....proclaiming the Lordship of Jesus Christ discipling the nations

A PEOPLE FOR GOD

We are a non denominational prayer and discipleship ministry. We are a prophetic army with an Apostolic mandate to proclaim and manifest the Lordship of Jesus as the Christ with full expressions of the kingdom of God amongst us and to make disciples of nations in every place. Our passion for God is deep Our vision is clear Our goal is Christ, to be like him in all things. Our experience with God is real and personal. We grow in Christ in all things We live as a family We work as a team We are the circumcision who worship God in the spirit and have no confidence in the flesh We believe in the manifestations of the sons of God to salvage the creation from the bondage of corruption. preparing the bride-The Church for Christ and enthroning Jesus Christ passion to see whole cities and nations turn to Christ.

THE CALL

Romans 10:13-15 For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved. How then shall they call on him in whom they have not believed? and how shall they believe in him of whom they have not heard? and how shall they hear without a preacher? And how shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace, and bring glad tidings of good things! At TMI, we desire that everyone discover their ministry and fulfill it. We raise, equip and prepare everyone through discipleship.

THE ASSIGNMENT

Acts 26:18, To open their eyes, and to turn them from darkness to light, and from the power of Satan unto God, that they may receive forgiveness of sins, and inheritance among them which are sanctified by faith that is in Christ. We have a lifetime assignment to turn sinners and whole cities to Christ. We are committed to bringing believers into spiritual maturity, sonship inheiritance and conformity to the image of Jesus Christ.
