About Us

PREPARING THE WAY FOR THE COMING OF THE LORD

WHO WE ARE

We are so blessed to have you visit us at Twainman Ministry Intl. TMI is a prayer and discipleship ministry. We exist to proclaim the Lordship of Jesus to the nations, and fulfill their ordination and ministry. At TMI we have a goal to bring men into their sonship inheritance. We are non- denominational and we embrace everyone regardless of age, race, color or tribe. We believe in miracles. We are non-denominational and we embrace everyone regardless of age, race or any other characteristic.

You do not need to be perfect to fellowship with us at TMI, just be willing to learn and grow. We are committed to ensuring you grow in knowledge by the Word and lead a life of prayer. At TMI we are a strong family system giving all who come a sense of belonging, we love, we care, we support and we build up. We look forward to having you in fellowship with us.

OUR MISSION

To proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ and make disciples of all nations, teaching them to observe all that He has commanded us with love and compassion.

To restore the fullness of God's kingdom on earth through prayer, worship, and demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit in every aspect of life.

To serve our community with excellence, reaching the lost, healing the broken, and empowering believers to walk in their God-given purpose and calling.
